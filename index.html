How I Met Sama

So as usual in my boring life, playing Valorant all the time while being sad, I met this one girl named Sama. At first, I didn't feel anything, but after some time, when we started talking, I got to know her and shared all my past with her. And then, when I started understanding how amazing, loyal, and sweet she is, I had this strange feeling developing for her...

Within no time, I had a big crush on her. I mean, she is soooooo beautiful, soooo smart, soooo cute, and most importantly, she understands me. Unlike others, she was different. One day, she seemed a little sad, and without any plan, I took a random train to Mumbai just to see her...

When I saw her in real life for the first time... OOO MY GOD! It felt like I was in heaven. Deep in my heart, I just wanted to stare at her forever. We went on an adventure together, from riding a scooter to going on a giant wheel where I couldn't take my eyes off her. Every moment felt magical...

But then, things took a turn. A misunderstanding created distance between us. I tried calling, texting, even praying for things to go back to normal. The truth is, I don't expect anything from her. I just want her to be happy and to be in my life, no matter what...
